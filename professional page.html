Job Description

A programmer uses various programming languages to develop code that a computer can comprehend and execute. This encompasses the maintenance, debugging, and troubleshooting to ensure the program meets the required standard.

Qualifications

In many cases, a bachelor's degree in computer science, software engineering, or any IT-related field is required to become a programmer. However, some employers may accept a certificate along with relevant experience. The most important factor is having the necessary skills to work as a programmer.
